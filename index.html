Welcome to JIC Official Website

At JIC, Midhakur, we are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and supportive learning environment where every child feels valued and respected. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and a lifelong love of learning. Our dedicated faculty and staff work collaboratively to inspire students to reach their full potential, both academically and personally.

About our School

Janta Inter College, Midhakur, Agra was established in 1965. It is a government aided college affiliated to UP Board, Prayagraj.

Our Mission

We are committed to develop our students as disciplined and highly proficient in their respective areas of study. Our aim is to make them responsible and educationally well being citizens of this country. Our entire teaching as well as non teaching staff members are putting their best efforts to create an amiable and cooperative environment for the students to facilitate them with smooth and speedy learning. Parents and teachers meetings are organized frequently in order to have a continuous dialogue with parents to monitor progress of the students. Together we shall rise to greater heights. Jai Hind
